My mission


With a data-driven and risk management approach, I focus on the following main areas:
► IT Asset Management (SAM, HAM, CMDB, SaaS Discovery, Tagging strategies, Vulnerabilities Management)
► Cloud FinOps (Cost engineering optimisation, Chargeback, Accountability & Awareness, Automation & Integration into workflows)

This risk management approach helps companies handle several types of risks, prioritizing based on initial audit of customer scope & strategy.
These risks include, but not limited to:
➊ Financial risk: Software audits & regularization, overspending/licenses on the shelf, Unexpected pikes in cloud services cost, Non-optimised cloud costs, Non budgeted cost linked to a security breach (+ reputation brand damage)
➋ Cybersecurity risk: IAM - High privilege/Privilege escalation/Permissive policies, SOD, MFA - Social engineering campaigns, Vulnerabilities management - Outdated software/Bad configs/Malicious packages, Shadow IT, 3rd Party outage/leak
➌ Compliance risk: Software licensing, IT contractual copyright-related clauses, GDPR, ISO27001, SOC2, PCI DSS
